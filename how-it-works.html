Our Process

How Our Virtual Dress Modeling Service Works

A simple, streamlined workflow that transforms your product-only photos into professional, model-worn visuals — powered by cutting-edge AI technology.

1 STEP ONE

Upload Your Product Images

Start by sending us your product-only images. These can be flat lays, hanger shots, mannequin photos, or any clean product images you already have. We accept all standard formats — JPEG, PNG, TIFF — in any resolution. The better the original product photo quality, the more stunning the final result. You can upload through our secure portal or simply email them to our team.

Supported: Clothing, jewelry, footwear, accessories, eyewear, hats, and more.

Upload Interface — Drag & drop your product photos
2 STEP TWO

AI Virtual Model Generation

Our proprietary AI engine analyzes your product images and generates photorealistic virtual models wearing your items. The technology understands fabric draping, lighting, shadows, and body proportions to create visuals that look indistinguishable from a real photoshoot. We match model demographics, poses, and styling to your brand identity and target audience.

For clothing items, we generate up to 3 different poses per product to give you variety for your marketing materials.

AI Processing — Virtual model generation in progress
3 STEP THREE

Receive Campaign-Ready Visuals

Within 48–72 hours, you receive your final images — professionally composed and ready to use across all your channels. Each image is delivered in multiple sizes optimized for e-commerce listings, social media, print catalogs, and advertising. We include high-resolution originals plus web-optimized versions so you're covered for every use case.

Need revisions? We offer revision rounds included in every plan to ensure you're 100% satisfied with the results.

Delivery — Campaign-ready images in multiple formats
